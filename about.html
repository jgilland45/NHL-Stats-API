This website is powered by the NHL API, found here. My website pulls data from this API, and then stores the data into multiple arrays. This data is then presented to you, the user, in ways that are much easier to read than lines and lines of numbers. I decided to make this website because I am very interested in NHL stats, as well as the NHL API and what it can do. Also, I'd like to give a huge thanks to Nick Kinlen, Faraz Kelhini, and Drew Hynes for their resources. They really helped me understand the ins and outs of the NHL API, and without them, I could not have completed this project.
